Project 1

HUMAN FOLLOWING ROBOT

One of the most exciting projects in my portfolio is the development of a human-following robot. This cutting-edge technology combines my expertise in robotics, computer vision, and artificial intelligence. The primary objective of this project was to create a robot that can autonomously track and follow a human being, making it an ideal candidate for applications in fields like security, healthcare, and even entertainment. To accomplish this, I integrated a range of sensors, including cameras, LiDAR, and ultrasonic sensors, which allowed the robot to perceive its environment and recognize the presence of a human. Advanced computer vision algorithms were employed to detect and track the human's movements in real-time. The robot's control system then used this information to calculate a suitable path and navigate to follow the human, maintaining a safe distance and avoiding obstacles in its path.
